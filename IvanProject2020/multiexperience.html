Multi-experience

Multi-experience development platforms (MXDPs) help enterprises to rapidly scale their applications across a range of devices and platforms.

MXDPs can help you develop a single application that can be deployed on a variety of devices.

This greatly shortens down your efforts and gives brands more control over their digital experiences.
